The Science

Your body develops and functions according to the interactions between proteins. By understanding the intricacies of one's DNA, one can optimize for certain lifestyle objectives.

Fat Loss

The precise way your body burns, or metabolizes, fats are unique because of your individual genetics. By investigating the functional effectiveness of your genes involved in breakdown, storage and processing of food, we can provide a detailed nutritional and exercise framework for you to shed weight faster than ever before.

Toning

Your genes that determine neuromuscular efficiency determine how muscle fibers should be maximally contracted at a given time without risking injury. We analyze the genetic dynamics of these muscular strength-building processes to design a regimen that can make you stronger than ever before.

Muscle Gain

Your muscles grow when the fibers are damaged and subsequently repaired, growing larger in the process. Numerous genes and proteins mediate and regulate this repair while others digest and process the nutrients your muscles need to grow. By analyzing your specific genetics, we can determine ideal exercise and nutritional strategies for you to rapidly increase muscle mass.

Privacy

Your genetic profile is your most personal information, and we will always keep that information safe and secure in the following ways:

  • HIPPA compliance - Under GINA, your HMO and other physical care rates will never increase based on the results of a genetic test.
  • Military-grade SHA1 and MD5 encryption of genomic data and data-transfer protocols.
  • Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) and AES-256 bit encryption.
  • Gene Guru applications and infrastructure are regularly tested for security vulnerabilities and hardened to enhance security and protect against attacks.
  • Gene Guru is designed with multiple layers of protection, including secure data transfer, encryption, network configuration, and application- and user-level controls that are distributed across a scalable, secure infrastructure. State-of-the-art industry standards of encryptions are constantly implemented to keep your data safe.
  • Access via third-party apps - Some third party apps with Gene Guru integration will ask for permission to access your Gene Guru. To grant access, you will need to log in to your Gene Guru account and explicitly press the Authorize button when prompted. You can visit the My Apps section of your account settings to review the apps you've granted permission to and revoke access at your discretion.
  • All sequencing executed in CLIA labs - CLIA is a widely accepted national standard of certification of the integrity and consistency of laboratory procedures.
  • We promise to never share your data. - We will make good on your trust in us by always keeping your data protected within our systems. We use a number of physical, technical, and heuristic security measures to protect user information from unauthorized access. No one else will ever be granted access to it.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What if my plan recommends a step which I am unable to complete?

Our plans are designed to help you reach your goal while living the life you want to live. To that end, you can choose not to undertake any suggested action, and your plan will automatically adjust to accommodate your choice.

2. How can I keep track of my progress?

Our user interface, in addition to offering a robust plan to take you to your goal, comes with tools for measuring your progress. A line graph shows you how much you are improving on a day-to-day basis, offers you suggestions on how to improve, and can use your past progress to predict your rate of progress in the future.

3. How long until I start seeing results?

Because our plans are genetically based and action-oriented, most people are able to see progress within the first 1 to 2 weeks of following their plan.

4. What do I do if my progress plateaus?

Part of Gene Guru’s progress tool is a built-in program for breaking plateaus. Your plan will automatically adjust to help you continue making progress toward your goal.

5. What if I want to change my goal?

Simply clicking the “change goal” button allows you to select any goal you wish to pursue, and your plan will automatically adjust to help you reach that goal as quickly as possible.

6. Do I run any risks by joining Gene Guru?

Gene Guru is commitmented to your privacy: we go to great lengths to protect your genetic data, and will never sell it to anyone. Furthermore, if you are uncomfortable with anything that Gene Guru recommends, you have the option to remove the objectionable recommendation from your plan; your plan will adjust accordingly to get you the maximum results while keeping you feeling comfortable.

7. What if I already have my genetic information?

If you have already done genetic testing, you can get started with your Gene Guru plan as soon as it is released. Simply sign up and upload the report when we launch.

8. How long until I get my Report?

We will begin distributing genetic testing kits in October. After you send your kit back to us for processing, you will receive an email with a link to your comprehensive dashboard via email within 3 to 4 weeks.
